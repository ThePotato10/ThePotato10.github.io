Privacy Policy

This app collects no data on the user. No Google Analytics, no ads, nothing

You geolocation data, in the form of coordinates, will be used in order for the app to function, and if you opt out, the app will no longer work as intended. If you opt out, the coordinates will default to Washington DC, where this app was developed. This geolocation data is not tied to you, and is never stored or visible to the developer or any service the developer uses to program the app.
